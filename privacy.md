## Analytics and Tracking

This site uses two analytics services to understand traffic patterns and improve content. Neither is used for advertising.

### Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

This site uses **Google Analytics 4**, operated by Google LLC

GA4 collects:
- Pages visited and time spent
- Approximate geographic location (country/city level, derived from IP)
- Browser type, operating system, and device category
- Referring source (how you found this site)

GA4 uses cookies and similar technologies to distinguish returning visitors. Data is sent to Google's servers and may be stored in the United States. Google may also use this data per its own [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Default data retention in GA4 is set to **14 months**.

You can opt out of GA4 tracking by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) or by using a browser with tracking protection enabled.

### Ahrefs Analytics

This site uses **Ahrefs Analytics**, operated by Ahrefs Pte. Ltd.

Ahrefs Analytics collects similar page-view and referral data to help understand SEO traffic sources. It does not set persistent cookies. Data is processed per [Ahrefs' Privacy Policy](https://ahrefs.com/privacy).
